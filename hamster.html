Do you know how many hamsters are there in the world totally？

Answer：About 32,000,000!(Estimating the global number of hamsters is challenging due to the many species and their varied habitats. There are approximately 12 million wild hamsters globally, though this number could be higher since wild populations are difficult to count. In captivity, which is easier to track, there are roughly 20 million hamsters kept as pets worldwide.)
